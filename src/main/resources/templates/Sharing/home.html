Ceramic Coat

A ceramic coating (which it is commonly referred to) is a liquid chemical which bonds to your vehicle’s paintwork forming a strong, durable protective layer which is stronger than traditional waxes and sealants. A ceramic coating application as part of a paint correction detail or new car protection detail will repel water, dirt, road salt and be resistant to harsh detergents and chemicals.
